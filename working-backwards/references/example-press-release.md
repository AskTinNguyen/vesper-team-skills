# Example Press Release

## Headline
Vesper Introduces Trusted Peer Agents for Secure Team Follow-Ups

## Subheadline
Trusted Peer Agents help managers and operators delegate recurring coordination work to their own AI agents while keeping every action permissioned and auditable.

## Dateline
San Francisco - March 1, 2028

## Opening Paragraph
Vesper today announced Trusted Peer Agents, a new way for teams to coordinate through their own personal AI agents without giving up control. Teams can now collect updates, send follow-ups, and coordinate routine requests through trusted agent-to-agent workflows while keeping human approval where it matters. The result is less status chasing, fewer interruptions, and a clear record of every decision.

## Customer Problem (Before)
Before Trusted Peer Agents, managers and operators had to manually chase teammates for updates, repeat the same reminder requests, and stitch fragmented responses into one decision-ready view.

## New Experience (After)
Now a manager can define the request once, let Vesper coordinate routine follow-ups in the background, and review only the requests that need approval. Every interaction is visible, revocable, and tied to a clear owner. Teams spend less time managing coordination overhead and more time acting on the result.

## Example Use Case
An engineering manager asks Vesper to collect Friday status updates from five team leads. Vesper sends the requests, tracks responses, escalates only missing or ambiguous replies, and delivers one clean summary before the weekly review.

## Customer Quote
"I stopped spending half my Friday morning chasing updates. I still have control, but I no longer have to do the repetitive work myself." - Engineering Manager

## Team or Product Quote
"We built Trusted Peer Agents around one principle: your agent can help coordinate work, but never behind your back." - Product Lead

## Availability
Trusted Peer Agents will roll out first in beta for workspace admins with explicit peer trust enabled. File transfer and broad automation remain disabled during the initial rollout.

## Call to Action
Join the beta waitlist in Vesper settings to enable Trusted Peer Agents for your workspace.

## Why This Is Strong
- Names the target user and their painful before-state.
- Makes trust and control explicit.
- Includes scope limits so the promise stays believable.
