## Clarifying Question Prompts

Ask one at a time when needed. Include your hypothesis.

- "Who is the primary user here? My hypothesis: ..."
- "What painful behavior should change if this succeeds? My hypothesis: ..."
- "What business result matters most, and by when? My hypothesis: ..."

- "What do we know already vs what are we assuming?"
