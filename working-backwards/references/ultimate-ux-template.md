### 2. Entry Moment

### 3. Core Flow
3-7 steps showing the happy path with minimal friction.

### 4. Decision or Approval Moment
Where risk is highest and how the product keeps control with the user.

### 5. Recovery or Edge Case
How the system handles uncertainty without breaking trust.

### 6. Outcome Moment
What the user has achieved by the end and why it feels better.
