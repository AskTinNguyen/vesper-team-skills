# Room Size Guidelines

## Residential Spaces - Vietnam/SEA Context

### Bedrooms

| Type | Minimum | Comfortable | Luxury |
|------|---------|-------------|--------|
| Master Suite | 16 sqm | 20-25 sqm | 30+ sqm |
| Master (bed only) | 12 sqm | 14-18 sqm | 20+ sqm |
| Secondary Bedroom | 10 sqm | 12-14 sqm | 16+ sqm |
| Children's Room | 9 sqm | 12-14 sqm | 16+ sqm |
| Guest Room | 10 sqm | 12-14 sqm | 16+ sqm |
| Helper's Room | 6 sqm | 8-10 sqm | 12+ sqm |

**Notes:**
- Master suite includes en-suite bathroom and walk-in closet
- Add 2-3 sqm for built-in wardrobe if not walk-in
- Vietnamese families often prefer larger master bedrooms

### Living Areas

| Type | Minimum | Comfortable | Luxury |
|------|---------|-------------|--------|
| Living Room | 18 sqm | 25-35 sqm | 40+ sqm |
| Family Room | 15 sqm | 20-25 sqm | 30+ sqm |
| Combined Living/Dining | 30 sqm | 40-50 sqm | 60+ sqm |
| Home Office | 8 sqm | 12-15 sqm | 20+ sqm |

**Notes:**
- Consider furniture layout (sofa, TV distance)
- TV viewing distance: 1.5-2.5x screen diagonal
- Allow 0.9m minimum circulation paths

### Dining Areas

| Seating | Minimum | Comfortable |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| 4 persons | 8 sqm | 10-12 sqm |
| 6 persons | 10 sqm | 14-16 sqm |
| 8 persons | 14 sqm | 18-20 sqm |
| 10+ persons | 18 sqm | 24+ sqm |

**Table sizes:**
- 4-seater: 120x80 cm
- 6-seater: 160x90 cm
- 8-seater: 200x100 cm
- Round 6-seater: 120 cm diameter

### Kitchen

| Type | Minimum | Comfortable | Professional |
|------|---------|-------------|--------------|
| Galley | 6 sqm | 8-10 sqm | - |
| L-shaped | 8 sqm | 12-14 sqm | 16+ sqm |
| U-shaped | 10 sqm | 14-16 sqm | 20+ sqm |
| Island Kitchen | 14 sqm | 18-22 sqm | 25+ sqm |

**Work triangle:** Sum of distances between sink, stove, refrigerator should be 4-8 meters

### Bathrooms

| Type | Minimum | Comfortable | Luxury |
|------|---------|-------------|--------|
| Half Bath (WC + sink) | 1.5 sqm | 2-3 sqm | 4+ sqm |
| Full Bath (no tub) | 3 sqm | 4-5 sqm | 6+ sqm |
| Full Bath (with tub) | 5 sqm | 6-8 sqm | 10+ sqm |
| Master Bath | 6 sqm | 8-12 sqm | 15+ sqm |

**Fixture clearances:**
- Toilet: 75cm width, 45cm front clearance
- Sink: 60-90cm width
- Shower: 80x80cm minimum, 90x90cm comfortable
- Bathtub: 170x75cm standard

### Utility Spaces

| Type | Minimum | Comfortable |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Laundry | 3 sqm | 5-7 sqm |
| Storage Room | 2 sqm | 4-6 sqm |
| Walk-in Closet | 4 sqm | 6-10 sqm |
| Pantry | 2 sqm | 4-6 sqm |

### Outdoor Spaces

| Type | Minimum | Comfortable |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Balcony | 4 sqm | 8-12 sqm |
| Terrace | 10 sqm | 20-30 sqm |
| Covered Patio | 15 sqm | 25-40 sqm |

### Circulation

- Hallways: 90cm minimum, 120cm comfortable
- Stairs: 90cm minimum width, 100-120cm comfortable
- Doorways: 80cm minimum, 90cm standard
- Entry Foyer: 4 sqm minimum, 6-10 sqm comfortable

## Ceiling Heights

| Space | Minimum | Standard | Luxury |
|-------|---------|----------|--------|
| Living Areas | 2.7m | 3.0m | 3.5m+ |
| Bedrooms | 2.5m | 2.7m | 3.0m+ |
| Bathrooms | 2.4m | 2.5m | 2.7m+ |
| Kitchen | 2.5m | 2.7m | 3.0m |

## Vietnamese Villa Considerations

- **Altar room/space:** Often required, 4-6 sqm, prominent position
- **Multi-generational living:** Consider separate entrances or wings
- **Helper quarters:** Common in villas, with separate entrance
- **Wet kitchen vs dry kitchen:** Dual kitchen setup popular
- **Garage/parking:** 15 sqm per car minimum
- **Garden/courtyard:** Important for natural ventilation and light
