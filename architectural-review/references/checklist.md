## Light & Ventilation

### Natural Light
- [ ] All habitable rooms have windows
- [ ] Living areas well-lit
- [ ] Bedrooms have adequate but controllable light
- [ ] Dark internal spaces minimized

### Airflow
- [ ] Cross-ventilation possible
- [ ] Kitchens and bathrooms ventilated
- [ ] Bedrooms have operable windows

---

## Feng Shui Quick Check

### Entry (Critical)
- [ ] No direct line to back door
- [ ] Stairs don't face entrance directly
- [ ] Bathroom not visible from entry
- [ ] Welcoming foyer space

### Bedroom (Critical)
- [ ] Bed can be in command position
- [ ] Solid wall available for headboard
- [ ] En-suite door doesn't face bed directly
- [ ] No beams over bed area

### Kitchen (Important)
- [ ] Stove and sink not directly opposite
- [ ] Cook can potentially see entrance
- [ ] Not adjacent to bathroom

### General
- [ ] No long straight corridors
- [ ] Sharp corners minimized
- [ ] Bathrooms not in center of home

---

## Practical Considerations

### Storage
- [ ] Bedroom closets adequate
- [ ] Kitchen storage sufficient
- [ ] Linen/utility storage exists
- [ ] Entry storage (shoes, coats)

### Utilities
- [ ] Plumbing efficiently stacked
- [ ] Electrical panel accessible
- [ ] HVAC can be accommodated
- [ ] Water heater location sensible

### Future-Proofing
- [ ] Aging-in-place considerations
- [ ] Technology infrastructure (conduits)
- [ ] Potential for modification

## Red Flags ðŸš©

Watch for these common issues:

1. **Bedroom doors at end of long hallway** (feng shui concern)
2. **Bathroom in center of floor plan** (no natural vent)
3. **Kitchen isolated from dining** (poor flow for serving)
4. **Master bedroom over garage** (noise, fumes)
5. **Stairs as only way between main spaces** (circulation bottleneck)
6. **No transition from outside to inside** (missing foyer)
7. **Bedroom directly off living room** (privacy)
8. **All bathrooms on one side** (plumbing not optimized)

## Questions to Ask Designer

1. What's the rationale for [specific layout choice]?
2. How is ventilation addressed in [specific room]?
3. Where would furniture be placed in [room]?
4. What materials are proposed for [area]?
5. How does this compare to the client's brief?
6. What alternatives were considered for [area]?
