# Vesper Team Skills — Cold Outreach Messages
## *"Selling Feelings, Not Features"*

---

## PRINCIPLES FOR ALL OUTREACH

1. **Lead with the feeling**, not the product
2. **Name their pain** in emotional terms
3. **Paint the transformation** — before/after feelings
4. **Make it about them**, not about us
5. **One clear CTA** — low friction, high curiosity

## FOLLOW-UP RULES

1. **Never follow up more than 3 times**
2. **Each follow-up adds value**, never just "checking in"
3. **Acknowledge their silence** — "I know you're busy"
4. **Make it easy to say no** — "Totally fine if this isn't a priority"
5. **Leave the door open** — "Feel free to reach out if things change"

---

## KEY PHRASES TO USE

✅ "The feeling of..."
✅ "What if you could..."
✅ "Imagine if your team..."
✅ "Not just faster, but..."
✅ "The difference between..."
✅ "You've probably felt..."
✅ "What that feels like..."

❌ Avoid: "Our product has..." "We offer..." "Features include..."

---

## MEASUREMENT

Track not just reply rates, but *sentiment*:
- Are people saying "tell me more" or "not interested"?
- Are they asking about feelings/outcomes or features/pricing?
- Are they forwarding to teammates?

**The goal:** Messages that make people *feel* understood.
