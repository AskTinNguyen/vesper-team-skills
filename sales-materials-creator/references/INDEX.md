## 🎨 Create Beautiful Decks with Gamma

These markdown files are designed to work seamlessly with **[Gamma](https://gamma.app)** — the AI-powered presentation tool.

### Quick Workflow:

1. **Copy** the content from `01-sales-deck.md` or `02-pitch-deck.md`
2. **Paste** into [gamma.app](https://gamma.app) → "Create New" → "Paste in text"
3. **Select** a template that matches your brand
4. **Generate** → Gamma creates beautiful slides automatically
5. **Refine** → Edit visuals, add images, adjust layouts

### Why Gamma?

- ✅ **Preserves structure** — Headers become slide titles, content becomes bullets
- ✅ **AI-powered design** — Professional layouts without design work
- ✅ **Easy customization** — Change themes, colors, fonts in one click
- ✅ **Export anywhere** — PDF, PPTX, or shareable link
- ✅ **Collaborative** — Team editing and comments

### Pro Tips:

- Use the **"Document"** mode in Gamma for narrative flow
- Use the **"Presentation"** mode for slide-by-slide delivery
- The `## SLIDE X:` headers translate perfectly to Gamma's slide structure
- Add images for the "Tuesday 2:47 PM" scenarios to make them memorable

## 🎭 Core Feelings We Sell

### The Transformation

| Before (Pain) | After (Pleasure) |
|---------------|------------------|
| 😰 Anxious about tribal knowledge | 😌 Confident, knowledge encoded |
| 😤 Frustrated, reinventing wheels | 🚀 Energized, reusing best practices |
| 😔 Disappointed in quality | 💪 Proud of consistent excellence |
| 😵 Overwhelmed onboarding | 🤝 Welcoming, fast ramp-up |
| 😞 Paralyzed by tool choices | 🧘 In flow, effortless execution |

### Key Feeling Words

**Use these in every conversation:**
- Confident, Capable, Proud
- Aligned, Flow, Effortless
- Clear, Empowered, Natural
- Masterful, Certain, Light

## 📞 Call Structure (The FEELS Framework)

1. **F**ocus on feelings — Start with emotional audit
2. **E**xplore their pain — Listen for emotional language
3. **E**cho back — Make them feel understood
4. **L**ift to future — Paint the transformed feeling
5. **S**ecure commitment — Close on feeling, not features

---

## ✉️ Outreach at a Glance

### Email Principles
- Lead with their pain, not our product
- Use feeling words in every sentence
- One clear CTA — low friction
- Maximum 3 follow-ups

### Subject Lines That Work
- "That feeling when your team just *knows*"
- "What if every engineer had superpowers?"
- "The gap between AI tools and AI workflows"

---

## 🚫 What NOT to Do

❌ Lead with "We have 54 skills..."
❌ Talk about features before feelings
❌ Use jargon without emotional context
❌ Pressure or create urgency artificially
❌ Make it about us, not them

---

## ✅ What TO Do

✅ Ask "How does that make you feel?"
✅ Use their words to reflect back
✅ Tell stories, not specifications
✅ Make it safe to say no
✅ Leave them better than you found them

---

## 📊 Measuring Success

### Qualitative (Leading)
- Do prospects use feeling words unprompted?
- Are they sharing specific team scenarios?
- Is there energy/excitement in conversations?

### Quantitative (Lagging)
- Demo-to-close rate
- Time to close
- Expansion revenue
- Customer satisfaction (feeling-based NPS)
