# Gamma Integration Guide
## *From Markdown to Beautiful Presentations*

---

## 🎯 What This Guide Covers

This guide shows you how to transform the markdown sales materials into professional presentations using **[Gamma](https://gamma.app)**.

**Time required:** 10-15 minutes  
**Result:** Polished, investor-ready deck

---

## 📋 Prerequisites

- Gamma account (free tier works) → [Sign up](https://gamma.app)
- Sales deck content from `01-sales-deck.md` or `02-pitch-deck.md`
- Your brand assets (logo, colors — optional)

---

## 🚀 Step-by-Step Workflow

### Step 1: Prepare Your Content

**Option A: Use the Full Deck**
1. Open `01-sales-deck.md` (sales deck) or `02-pitch-deck.md` (pitch deck)
2. Select all content (Cmd+A / Ctrl+A)
3. Copy (Cmd+C / Ctrl+C)

**Option B: Use Specific Slides**
1. Review the deck structure in the markdown
2. Copy only the slides you need
3. Each `## SLIDE X:` section becomes one Gamma slide

---

### Step 2: Import to Gamma

1. **Go to** [gamma.app](https://gamma.app)
2. **Click** "Create New AI" (big purple button)
3. **Select** "Paste in text" option
4. **Paste** your copied markdown content
5. **Click** "Continue"

---

### Step 3: Choose Your Format

Gamma offers three formats. For sales materials, we recommend:

| Format | Best For | Our Recommendation |
|--------|----------|-------------------|
| **Presentation** | Live pitching, slide-by-slide | ⭐ Use for investor pitches |
| **Document** | Reading, narrative flow | ⭐ Use for sales leave-behinds |
| **Webpage** | Sharing, async viewing | Use for one-pagers |

**For `01-sales-deck.md`:** Select **Document** or **Presentation**  
**For `02-pitch-deck.md`:** Select **Presentation**

---

### Step 4: Select a Theme

Choose a theme that matches your brand personality:

| Theme Style | Vibe | Best For |
|-------------|------|----------|
| **Minimal** | Clean, professional | B2B sales, enterprise |
| **Bold** | Confident, energetic | Startups, product demos |
| **Elegant** | Refined, trustworthy | Investor pitches, partnerships |
| **Playful** | Friendly, approachable | Community, developer tools |

**Recommended for Vesper Team Skills:** Minimal or Elegant

---

### Step 5: Generate & Review

1. **Click** "Generate" — Gamma creates your slides
2. **Review** each slide for:
   - ✅ Content accuracy
   - ✅ Visual hierarchy
   - ✅ Brand alignment
3. **Note** any slides that need adjustment

---

### Step 6: Refine & Customize

#### Editing Content

- **Click** any text to edit inline
- **Use** the AI assistant ( sparkle icon) to rewrite sections
- **Add** speaker notes in the notes panel (bottom of each slide)

#### Adding Visuals

For key slides, add images to enhance emotional impact:

| Slide | Suggested Visual |
|-------|------------------|
| SLIDE 1: The Opening | Stressed engineer at desk |
| SLIDE 4: The Quiet Moments | Split screen: before/after team |
| SLIDE 5: What You Actually Get | Icons representing skill categories |
| SLIDE 7: How It Works | Simple 3-step workflow diagram |

**Image sources:**
- Gamma's built-in Unsplash integration
- [Undraw.co](https://undraw.co) (free illustrations)
- Your own product screenshots

#### Adjusting Layouts

- **Click** the layout button (grid icon) to change slide layouts
- **Try:**
  - "Title + Content" for text-heavy slides
  - "Two Column" for before/after comparisons
  - "Image + Text" for story slides

#### Branding

1. **Click** "Theme" (top right)
2. **Set** your brand colors
3. **Upload** your logo
4. **Choose** your fonts

---

### Step 7: Add Interactive Elements (Optional)

Gamma supports:
- **Embed** → Add Loom videos, Figma files, Calendly links
- **Webpages** → Embed your GitHub repo or product demo
- **Charts** → Show traction metrics, growth curves

**Recommended additions:**
- Calendly embed on the final "Next Steps" slide
- GitHub repo link on the "What You Actually Get" slide
- Demo video on the "How It Works" slide

---

### Step 8: Export & Share

#### Export Options

| Format | Use Case | How To |
|--------|----------|--------|
| **PDF** | Email attachments, printing | Share → Download → PDF |
| **PPTX** | PowerPoint compatibility | Share → Download → PowerPoint |
| **Web Link** | Async sharing, tracking | Share → Get link |
| **Embed** | Website integration | Share → Embed code |

#### Sharing Settings

- **Public** — Anyone with link can view
- **Restricted** — Only specific emails can access
- **Password** — Link + password required

## 🎨 Slide-by-Slide Gamma Tips

### SLIDE 1: The Opening (Feel the Problem)

**Gamma Layout:** "Title + Full Image" or "Title Only"  
**Tip:** Use a dark, moody background image to set the "pain" tone  
**Text Treatment:** Large, bold question: *"How many times have you watched your team..."*

---

### SLIDE 2: The Emotional Transformation

**Gamma Layout:** "Comparison" or "Two Column"  
**Tip:** Left side = before (gray, muted), Right side = after (bright, vibrant)  
**Enhancement:** Add emoji icons as visual anchors

---

### SLIDE 3: The Outcome

**Gamma Layout:** "Three Cards" or "Features Grid"  
**Tip:** Each feeling (Empowered, Fast, Aligned, Proud) gets its own card with icon

---

### SLIDE 4: The Quiet Moments

**Gamma Layout:** "Story" or "Narrative"  
**Tip:** Use three separate slides for the three scenarios (Tuesday, Thursday, Friday)  
**Visual:** Clock icon showing time, then outcome screenshot

---

### SLIDE 5: What You Actually Get

**Gamma Layout:** "Table" or "Feature Grid"  
**Tip:** Use category icons (🎥 for Video, 💻 for Dev, etc.)  
**Enhancement:** Add hover tooltips with skill examples

---

### SLIDE 6: The Real ROI

**Gamma Layout:** "Before/After" or "Comparison"  
**Tip:** Use checkmarks ✅ and crosses ❌ for visual contrast  
**Text:** Emphasize the *feeling* in italics

---

### SLIDE 7: How It Works

**Gamma Layout:** "Process" or "Steps"  
**Tip:** Numbered 1-2-3 with connecting arrows  
**Enhancement:** Add code block styling for the CLI commands

---

### SLIDE 8-11: Closing Slides

**Gamma Layout:** "Call to Action" or "Title Only"  
**Tip:** Use your brand's primary color for emphasis  
**Must-have:** Clear next step (calendar link, email, phone)

## 💡 Pro Tips for Gamma Mastery

### 1. Use the AI Assistant

**Access:** Click the sparkle ✨ icon on any slide  
**Prompts that work well:**
- "Make this more concise"
- "Add a compelling headline"
- "Rewrite for emotional impact"
- "Suggest a better layout for this content"

### 2. Speaker Notes

**Add to:** Every slide  
**Content:** 
- Key talking points
- Transition phrases
- Feeling words to emphasize
- Questions to ask

**Example for SLIDE 2:**
> "Pause here. Ask: 'Which of these before feelings resonates most with your team?' Listen for their #1 pain point."

### 3. Mobile Preview

**Check:** How your deck looks on mobile  
**Why:** Investors often review on phones  
**Access:** Share → Preview → Mobile

### 4. Version Control

**Save versions as you iterate:**
- v1 — Initial import
- v2 — Content refined
- v3 — Visuals added
- v4 — Final polish

**Access:** File → Version History

### 5. Collaboration

**Add teammates:**
- Sales reps for feedback
- Designer for visual polish
- Founder for final approval

**Use comments:** Highlight text → Add comment
